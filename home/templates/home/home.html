Journey

Here's what led me to becoming the Software Engineer I'm today

  • 2017-2019

    Humble Beginnings

    It all started thanks to my high school's Game Design Club (of which I became the president in my last year!). My programming journey started with making small games in PhaserJS and later Unity C#.

  • 2019-2022

    Swansea University

    I decided to embark on an adventure to study abroad. I did my Computer Science B.Sc. in the beautiful region of South Wales. I graduated with First Class Honours! PS I got to work as a programming teaching assistant.

  • Mar 2021 - May 2021

    Machine Learning with DataWorkshop

    Covid hit, with the spare time on my hands and a growing passion for machine learning I decided to join a bootcamp led by DataWorkshop. I took part in my first Kaggle competition and got a fourth place!

  • Summer 2021

    Michelin Data Analyst Intern

    My first position in data. After working with Excel, PowerBI, and Oracle I decided to steer my career towards software engineering. But hey! I unlocked a secret talent - knowing how to make a tyre.

  • Nov 2021 - Jun 2022

    Booksy Python Developer Intern

    In a team with two other brilliant interns, and the massive support from our Booksy mentors, I got to learn all about backend engineering and Django.

  • Sep 2022 - to Present

    THG Software Engineer

    I joined the experimentation team in THG to expand the A/B testing capabilities, and develop an A/B testing platform as an enterprise offering. Details of projects are described in the portfolio section.
